Our process

How we work
01
Discovery

We listen deeply to understand your vision, lifestyle, and the feeling you want your space to evoke. This phase is less about architecture and more about people.

Initial consultation & site visit
Lifestyle & aesthetic questionnaire
Budget scoping & timeline planning
02
Concept

A full creative direction distilled into one cohesive vision — mood boards, spatial layouts, material palettes. You see the future before a single wall is touched.

Mood boards & visual direction
Spatial layout concepts
Material & colour palette selection
03
Design

Detailed drawings, specifications, and curated selections crafted with precision. Every element chosen with intentionality — nothing arbitrary, nothing generic.

Technical drawings & specifications
Furniture & fixture sourcing
Contractor briefing & coordination
04
Realisation

We oversee every detail of execution, ensuring the final space matches the vision we set together. You walk in on reveal day and feel exactly what we promised.

On-site project management
Quality control & sign-off
Final styling & photography
